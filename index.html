About This Library

This site helps DCS F-4E Phantom II pilots memorize PRF tones for the ALR-46's Handoff mode. Audio is taken from the DCS Heatblur module.

Radar Modes You’ll Hear

  • Search: Radar is scanning for targets
  • Acquisition: Radar is attempting to lock on
  • Track: Radar is actively tracking you

Certain systems like the SA-2 Fan Song and SA-3 Low Blow may skip acquisition depending on range/aspect. Only radars detectable by the ALR-46 are included.
