About This Library

This site is designed to help DCS F-4E Phantom II pilots learn and memorize the PRF (Pulse Repetition Frequency) tones for the ALR-46's Handoff mode. The audio files are extracted directly from the DCS Heatblur F-4E module to ensure authenticity.
